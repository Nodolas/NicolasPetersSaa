Projects I have done so far

PIPRA

Pipra Health AG is a pioneering health-tech company focused on improving patient outcomes through predictive analytics. Their product is a Software-as-a-Medical-Device (SaMD) designed to predict postoperative delirium based on patient records, helping healthcare providers take proactive measures to enhance patient safety. I built and determined the regulatory pathway for their product, ensuring proper classification and compliance.

dianovi

Dianovi is an AI-driven company based in Darmstadt, focused on revolutionizing emergency room care with advanced artificial intelligence. Their technology assists clinicians in decision-making, ensuring that every patient receives the appropriate care by enhancing diagnostics through anamnesis and patient records. I played a key role in developing a 13485-compliant Quality Management System (QMS) to support their medical technology compliance and regulatory requirements.

Now I am working on AI-Agents

By combining my expertise in digital innovation and consulting, I create intelligent, AI-powered tools that enhance decision-making, streamline processes, and generate high-impact results, empowering consultants to deliver value to their clients.
